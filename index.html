Kamu's Offering

  • 2D platformer, metroidvania
  • Control of a sphere as main mechanic
  • Setting developed in Africa
  • I work side by side with my partner Pedro Núñez Ribot
  • In development process. We start from the completion of a Vertical Slice, on the way to its final publication

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Kamu's Offering tells a love story. You are entrusted with the task of gathering the fragments of the heart of the god "Kamu" with the aim of delivering them to his beloved, whom he had to abandon long ago to protect his people.

The sphere with which the gameplay is developed represents the essence of this heart and the fragments that you find suppose new abilities: Throw, bounce, collect, dash, special states, etc.

The adventure takes place on the African continent, drinking from its culture, its places, its symbols, voodoo ...

Three worlds to explore (desert, mountain and jungle)
Twelve levels full of new mechanics (temples, natural landscapes, fantastic settings)
More than ten hours of gameplay.

POP. 1280

  • Narrative game, genre noir
  • Exploring the environment in a detective environment, searching for clues and solving puzzles
  • Set in spanish civil postwar, in a northern town
  • Developed up to Vertical Slice

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Pop. 1280 tells the story based in the 50s and is about a civil guard who makes his route through the towns of northern Spain. In constant communication with his fellow barracks via radio, he runs into several bloody crimes and a plot in which nothing is what it seems.

We work the narrative based on the "unreliable narrator" or unreliable narrator. What the player perceives and what the protagonist perceives follow different aspects, we seek a disconnection between player and protagonist, since the reasons for this fictional character are different from those expressed to the player and little by little he will carry out actions based on your own intentions. We take the player through an experience where it is discovered that the protagonist is the author of a whole plan that revolves around his own benefit.

Purely physical interactions with the environment, 100% diegetic elements (no virtual interface, no minimap, story told by voice, etc.)

Gameplay worked through exploration and puzzles, a bicycle that serves as transport and inventory, a diary where collect photographs and clues, etc.

MY ROLES

  • Narrative and audio implementation systems

Bullets are the new prayer

  • Top-Down Shooter
  • Action, tension and frenzy
  • Zombie hordes of many classes, various weapons and abilities
  • Set in a post-apocalyptic rural area
  • First consistent project, a 3-month production created by me

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Treated with a mild narrative in which a SWAT agent has the objective of finding his lost platoon. After advancing through a completely devastated town and fighting an infinity of undead, find the origin of evil, a kind of necromancer sorceress that has killed your men and uses them against you in a final battle.

Use of lighting to create tension: shadows, streetlights that turn on and off, attraction to continuity with light bulbs, and the use of the character's flashlight that provides visibility in the pointing direction.

Using three types of weapons (submachine gun, shotgun and rifle) and escape tools (attractor grenades, dash and melee colpe), you must fight your way through waves of all kinds of enemies throughout the town, until you reach the final boss.

Final Boss designed with attack phases in which he generates platforms of rot that obstruct the way, launches spheres of energy that damage you and summons your zombie squad as he enters a state of chrysalis, becoming invulnerable.
