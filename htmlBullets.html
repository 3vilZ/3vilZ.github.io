Bullets are
the new Prayer

  • Top-Down Shooter
  • Action, tension and frenzy
  • Zombie hordes of many classes, various weapons and abilities
  • Set in a post-apocalyptic rural area
  • My first major academic project, 3-month development

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Light narrative where a SWAT agent has the objective of finding his lost squad. After advancing through a devastated town and fighting an infinity of undead, he finds the origin of evil, a kind of necromancer sorceress that has killed his men and uses them against you in a final battle.

Use of ilumination to create suspense: shadows, streetlights that turn on and off, attraction to continuity with light bulbs, and the use of the character's flashlight that provides visibility in the pointing direction.

Three types of weapons (submachine gun, shotgun and rifle) and escape tools (taunt grenades, dash and melee hit), you must fight your way through waves of enemies cross the village.

Final boss designed with several attack phases: throw energy clusters, generate decay areas in the ground, and enter a chrysalis, becoming inmune and summoning your squad as zombies.
