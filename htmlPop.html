Pop. 1280

  • Narrative game, genre noir
  • Exploration in a detective environment, searching for clues and solving puzzles
  • Set in spanish civil postwar, in a northern town
  • Developed up to Vertical Slice

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Pop. 1280 tells the story based in the 50s about a civil guard who makes his route through villages of northern Spain. In constant communication with his fellow barracks via radio, he runs into several bloody crimes and a plot in which nothing is what it seems.

We work the narrative based on the "unreliable narrator": What the player perceives differs from what the character intends to do. We seek a disconnection between player and character, since the reasons for this fictional avatar are different from those expressed to the player, little by little he will carry out actions based on his own intentions. We take the player through an experience where it is discovered that the protagonist is the author of a whole plan that revolves around his own benefit.

Purely physical interactions with the environment, 100% diegetic elements (no virtual interface, no minimap, story told by voice...)

Gameplay worked through exploration and puzzles, a bicycle that serves as transport and inventory, a diary where collect photographs and clues...
