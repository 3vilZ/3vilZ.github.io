My other projects

Here are some of the projects I have most enjoyed developing

VEGGIE REICH

  • One week GameJam. Character controller and animation management experiment.
  • Gameplay based on exploration, platforming and combat.
  • 4 skills and multiple enemies with unique behaviours.
  • The objective is to find four keys, obtain the essential elements and defeat the final boss.
  • Metroidvania genre. Map constrained by obstacles and unlocked by the skills.
  • Thematic based on a veggie world threatened by "Baron Von Brocoli" and his followers.

DISHONORED/DOOM BLOCKOUT

  • Level design project. Challenge of introducing "Doom" gameplay into a "Dishonored" level.
  • Highlighting the vertical progression of the level, from the harbour to the old city center.
  • Blending spaces designed for stealth gameplay with enviromental puzles, hidden collectable areas and combat arenas.

HELLNITE

  • One week GameJam themed on "Home". In a twist on the concept of "Home", we take the idea to hell and transform comfort/peace into suffering/torture.
  • Local party minigames for four players, they are simple, treated with comedy and played using a single keyboard.
  • Some examples are "Killing Kitties", "Blowing Up Humans" or "Shooting Kids".
  • Satan goes on holiday and leaves his careless son in charge of hell, who keeps playing "Hellnite". The players take on the role of four little devils who must maintain the climate of horror that the dark lord desires.
