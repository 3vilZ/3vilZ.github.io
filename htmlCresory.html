CRESORY

The digital solution for kids to learn finance by playing!

  • Gamification project that introduces children to financial education
  • Levels with videos, flash games and quizzes
  • The application is embedded on a web server, available in English and Spanish
  • We currently have a 5 level MVP and a reward system with skins
  • You can try it here: sandbox-juego.cresory.com

ABOUT THE PROJECT

We are a small startup trying to enter the market, we have an innovative idea that covers a deficit in the education of children in many countries: finance.

Through various levels, we cover several topics on basic finance: The value of money, saving, investing and budget management.

Each level has three elements. A Video showing theoretical concepts, history, applications... A minigame to test what has been learnt, trying to introduce different mechanics and ways of playing. And finally a Quiz, where we reinforce the concepts that the user has least understood with variety and feedback.

The application has a system of virtual coins that can be obtained with good results in the activities, and that can be exchanged with skins for our character.

We are currently working on new levels and new features (such as daily events or a housing system). We are also trying to progress in the marketing section and we are trying to attend several cultural and startup events to get visibility and funding.
